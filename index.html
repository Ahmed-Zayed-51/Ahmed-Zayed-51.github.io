About

Who I am and how I create value with data

My name is Ahmed Fathy Zayed. I’m a Data Analyst & Business Intelligence Specialist with strong experience in information systems, operational reporting, and building analytics solutions that connect business goals to measurable outcomes. I help teams move from “data noise” to clarity by designing structured datasets, defining KPIs, and creating dashboards that are accurate, fast, and easy to understand.

I work end-to-end: starting from data extraction with SQL, cleaning and transforming data using Python and Power Query, building strong models, then delivering executive-ready dashboards with Power BI and data storytelling. I care about quality: clean logic, consistent definitions, and performance optimization. My work usually includes automation and validation checks to reduce manual errors, improve refresh reliability, and ensure stakeholders can trust every number in the report.

If you need KPI tracking, operational reporting, or modern BI dashboards, I can help you design a clean analytics workflow and deliver a practical solution that your team can actually use—daily.
